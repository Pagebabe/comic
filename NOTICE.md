## Creative and asset rights

Every published or generated creative asset must carry enough provenance to answer:

- who created or supplied it;
- what source material was used;
- which tool or model produced it;
- which person approved it;
- what rights or restrictions apply;
- whether it is a draft, review candidate, approved master, or archived item;
- which commit and evidence record bind the decision.

Unknown provenance means `REVIEW_REQUIRED` and blocks reuse, publication, training, and promotion to a master asset.

This repository contains public engineering and creative-process evidence.
