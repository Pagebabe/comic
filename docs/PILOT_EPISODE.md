# Pilot Episode: Die Entkommerzialisierungsgebühr

## Format

4 panels, vertical 9:16 comic video.

Images stay clean: no speech bubbles, no readable dialogue, no fake subtitles. Dialogue is for voice, subtitles and edit timing only.

## Core joke

Falk invents an anti-commercial fee that Rico has to pay in cash. Rico is too new to Berlin to know whether this is absurd. Kralle and Sami make it clear: yes, but also normal enough.

## Cast

- Rico Bassmann: innocent, too clean, confused but polite.
- Falk Reuter: soft moral scammer, KeepCup, huge keychain, friendly voice.
- Kralle: Görli-cat judge on the window ledge.
- Sami: deadpan Berlin reality check in the doorway.

## Panel Script

### Panel 1

Location: Ricos Zimmer im Haus Nebenwirkung

Action: Rico sits on the mattress with Mutti's Tupperware. Falk enters with KeepCup and a crooked official-looking paper.

Voice:

- Falk: Kurzer Hausmoment.
- Rico: Ist was kaputt?
- Falk: Nur das System.

### Panel 2

Location: Ricos Zimmer im Haus Nebenwirkung

Action: Falk presents the fee paper like a moral document. Rico tries to understand.

Voice:

- Falk: Das ist keine Miete.
- Falk: Das ist eine Entkommerzialisierungsgebühr.
- Rico: Aber ich bezahle sie mit Geld?

### Panel 3

Location: Fensterbank in Ricos Zimmer

Action: Kralle sits on the window ledge and judges Falk. Rico counts coins.

Voice:

- Rico: Und wofür ist die genau?
- Kralle: Der nimmt sogar Mäusen Miete.

### Panel 4

Location: Türrahmen von Ricos Zimmer

Action: Rico pays. Falk takes the cash. Sami appears in the doorway.

Voice:

- Rico: Ist das normal?
- Sami: Willkommen in Berlin.

## Production Notes

- Keep the panels simple: one gag, one emotion, one location.
- Rico must look more innocent than the room around him.
- Falk must look friendly, not openly evil.
- Kralle must remain physically cat-like, not a fantasy creature.
- Color should be louder than the earlier realistic tests: toxic green, club pink, warning red, electric cyan.
- Do not use real logos, readable papers, readable money text or speech bubbles.
